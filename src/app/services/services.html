VIMS TECHNO CRAFTS Desiccants

VIMS Techno Crafts (VTC) desiccant solutions are designed to actively remove excess moisture from containers and product packaging, ensuring goods remain dry, safe, and protected from moisture-related damage. Our desiccant bags absorb moisture from the surrounding air, helping to maintain controlled humidity levels inside poly bags, cartons, boxes, and shipping containers.

During storage and transportation, especially in long-distance and sea shipments, moisture intrusion is a common challenge. Fluctuating temperatures can lead to condensation, container rain, and cargo sweat—often resulting in corrosion, mold or mildew growth, unpleasant odors, damaged packaging, and compromised product quality. Preventive moisture control measures are therefore essential to ensure cargo reaches its destination in optimal condition.

We pioneered the introduction of calcium chloride–based, starch-supported in-box desiccants, redefining how moisture protection is applied within packaging. Through advanced engineering, high-performance packaging materials, and newly developed sealing technologies, VTC created a highly efficient moisture-absorbing solution that can be placed in direct contact with goods vulnerable to moisture damage—an application previously limited to conventional and less effective desiccants such as silica gel or clay.

Compared to traditional desiccants such as silica gel and clay, VTC desiccants offer significantly enhanced performance during extended transport cycles. Engineered for high efficiency, they provide long-lasting moisture protection even under extreme temperature variations, making them well-suited for intercontinental shipments and demanding storage environments.

With a moisture absorption capacity exceeding several times their dry weight, VTC desiccants continue to perform effectively for extended durations across a wide temperature range. In contrast, conventional desiccants like silica gel and clay reach saturation quickly and operate efficiently only within limited temperature conditions, making them insufficient for reliable moisture control during sea transport. By delivering superior absorption, broader application range, and consistent performance, VIMS Techno Crafts desiccants provide a dependable solution for protecting cargo, packaging, and products against moisture damage risks.

VIMS TECHNO CRAFTS Desiccants

VTC Desiccants are formulated using a carefully balanced combination of highly hygroscopic calcium chloride and a specialized binding agent. Calcium chloride efficiently absorbs moisture from the surrounding air, while the binder securely locks in the absorbed moisture. The use of fine calcium chloride powder instead of coarse flakes ensures better blending, faster absorption, and improved gel formation, resulting in superior performance.

Our advanced desiccant formulation converts absorbed moisture into a stable, leak-free gel, completely eliminating the risk of liquid spillage. This contained gel structure provides safe and reliable moisture control, protecting cargo and packaging from damage caused by condensation, corrosion, mold, and excess humidity.

To ensure maximum effectiveness and safety, equal importance is given to packaging quality and manufacturing standards. VTC Desiccants are produced in state-of-the-art facilities using high-performance packaging materials sourced from trusted suppliers. These materials meet strict technical requirements for sealing strength, vapor transmission rate, and water barrier performance, ensuring consistent protection during storage and transportation.
